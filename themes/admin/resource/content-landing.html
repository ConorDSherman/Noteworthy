Welcome

Welcome to Noteworthy

You don't have any posts to show on this site (yet), but don't worry. You can be up and running in no time at all. Be sure to take a look around the Administration pages and set up a few things in the Sites and Settings pages. Once you add your Evernote API string, you can select which notebooks should be read into the database. You can find more help information in each of the Adminstration pages, too.

If you have any questions at all, feel free to contact me directly.

Your Posts

Here you can see all of your posts in reverse chronological order. Sometimes there can be problems with the import from Evernote and, when that happens, you'll see a red button on the right side saying "Repair". You can click that button to repair just a single post. Pressing "Refresh" will do exactly what you expect; it will refresh that one post. If you've configured Noteworthy to use a cron task, then posts will automatically update themselves with the next refresh.

Your Admin Link

Save your admin panel link! This link [ADMINURL] is unique to you and your site. Even if you share this link with other people, they will not be able to log in and make changes to your site. Keep it in a safe place, and don't delete your browser cookies.
